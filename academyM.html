Lassana Flora Academy


"Our vision is to add value to our talented young men and women though proper training and guidance".


By conducting classes for more than three years, the Lassana Flora Academy has been able to provide the much needed knowledge to achieve professional status in floral designing and provide proper training to armature artists in Sri Lanka.
We have now become the leading floral academy in Sri Lanka because,

  • We are Sri Lanka’s only professional floral design academy with an international reputation.
  • Attained proven track record in creating successful florists both locally and internationally.
  • Our trainees have already found jobs at Lassana Flora and many other countries like the USA, Japan, Australia, Europe and the Middle East
  • Many have started or improved in the wonderful floral art of creative design as a hobby
  • Many have started their own business or taken existing business new heights

    • The Certificate in Floral Designing will be offered to those who successfully complete the written and practical exams. While offering internships for training we offer permanent jobs to talented students who successfully complete the course.

    Beginner's Course


    An intensive fast track professional course especially intended for those who cannot afford time measured in months to become professionals in floral designing


    What will I learn ?

    This course is suitable for complete beginners with personal or professional interest. Learn the theoretical fundamentals of floral design and perfect the art of creating many different arrangements in class by using the principles and elements of design and colour.Students are awarded a certificate by the Lassana Flora Academy upon successful completion of this course of 10 days, 30 hours.


    What is included ?

    Our trainers make floral designing fun to learn. Course fee includes all you need to create floral arrangements including fresh and artificial flowers, floral foam, ribbons and other accessories.


    What to bring ?

    Bring yourself with heaps of enthusiasm


    Duration and Time

    Two Weeks / 10 classes (Monday to Friday 9:00am - 12:00pm)

    Fast Track Course


    An intensive fast track professional course especially intended for those who cannot afford time measured in months to become professionals in floral designing


    What will I learn ?

    This flower design course covers everything from traditional to contemporary design styles. In one and half months you’ll master 25 classes of design on average. All the classes are conducted by very talented professional trainers who will help you design with flowers in quick time. Come and blossom in the floral industry in Sri Lanka with help from our experts.


    What is included ?

    Course includes theory and hands – on practical sessions with end of course exam. All you need to create a floral arrangement are included with fresh and artificial flowers, floral foam, vases, wires, ribbons and decorative items to make your designs beautiful. Theoretical knowledge is provided via multi-media presentations, tutorials and lectures.


    What to bring ?

    Bring yourself with new ideas and inspiration to create floral arrangements


    Duration and Time

    One and half months / 25 classes (Monday to Friday 9:00am - 12:00pm)

    Professional Course


    A professional course for those who want to make it to the industry. This is your passport for a successful career or an opportunity to launch your own business. With the commencement of Sri Lanka’s first ever floral design and art course 3 years ago, the Lassana Flora Academy has earned a reputation for pioneering the best and the most comprehensive Professional Floral Designing and Art Course in the floral industry of our country today.


    What will I learn ?

    This popular course mainly focuses on the wedding and décor fields. It is intended for those who want to be successful in the industry. Students who follow this course are trained in both theoretical and practical aspects of design and the art field. Further, students will have the unique opportunity to learn the best practices of making home arrangements using modern techniques and accessories.The highly recognized Certificate in Floral Designing will be offered to every student who successfully completes both written and practical exams.


    What to bring ?

    Bring yourself with heaps of enthusiasm.


    Duration and Time

    3 months / 24 classes 144 hours (Saturday - Sunday 9:00am - 4:00pm)
